Fit a classic logic-puzzle machine into your pocket. Inspired by retro handheld consoles, WhatsCode turns your iPhone into a personal code-breaking device. No flashy effects—just pure logic, a ticking timer, and the rush of cracking the secret sequence.

### Core Gameplay: 7 Attempts to Break the Code
Decode a secret 4-item sequence before time runs out.
- **Green intel**: correct item and correct position.
- **White intel**: correct item, wrong position.
- **No intel**: this item is not in the code at all.
Use every clue to narrow the field and become a top operative.

### 7 Unlockable Visual Themes
Who says logic has to be dull? Unlock seven visual themes and give every mission a fresh look:
- **Classic Colors**, **Pixel Fruit**, **Cute Cat**, **Cute Dog**
- **Fast Food**, **Fresh Fruit**, **Healthy Veggies**
Each theme ships with hand-picked icons for a completely different vibe.

### Local Dual-Player Mode
One player sets the code, the other cracks it.
- Face-to-face battles perfect for commutes, parties, or coffee-break rivalries.
- No internet required—one device is all you need.

![Dual Mode](/images/posts/whatscode/WhatsCode_8.png)

### Difficulty & Progression
- **Easy**: limited palette + positional hints. Beginner-friendly.
- **Normal**: full palette + positional hints. The standard challenge.
- **Hard**: full palette + aggregate-only hints. Hardcore brain-burner.
Climb the ranks from Recruit all the way to Legendary across 500 levels.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What if I run out of hints?
Hints regenerate over time as you play. You can also earn extra hints by leveling up in Solo mode.

### 2. How do I unlock new themes?
Keep playing Solo mode and climb levels. New themes unlock automatically when you hit the required level.

### 3. Does Dual mode need an internet connection?
Nope. Dual mode is entirely local—pass the device back and forth between players.

### 4. Is my data safe?
All progress and stats are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or upload any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
