# WhatsCode - Privacy Policy

## Important Statement

**WhatsCode is a fully offline logic-puzzle game. We want to be absolutely clear:**

- We **do not collect** any personally identifiable information.
- We **do not upload** any data to remote servers.
- All game progress, levels, and statistics are **stored locally on your device only** (via iOS UserDefaults).
- The app **does not require an internet connection** to use any feature.
- The app **does not request** camera, microphone, location, or contacts permissions.
- You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data.

## Detailed Privacy Policy

### 1. Data Collection Scope

WhatsCode **does not collect** any of the following:

- Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone, etc.)
- Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
- Network activity or location data
- Photos, camera footage, or microphone recordings

The app only stores the following anonymous game data locally:
- Current game level and progression
- Difficulty settings and theme unlock status
- Local gameplay statistics (wins, attempt counts, etc.)
- Tutorial completion flag

### 2. How Information Is Used

All locally stored data is used solely to:
- Save your game progress so you can resume later.
- Track theme unlocks and rank progression.
- Display personal gameplay statistics (local display only).

### 3. Third-Party Services & Data Disclosure

WhatsCode **does not integrate** any third-party analytics, advertising, or social SDKs.

We will not disclose your information to any third party except:
- When required by law, legal process, or government request.
- With your explicit consent.

### 4. Data Security

Even though all data remains on your device, we still take the following precautions:
- Game data is stored via the standard iOS UserDefaults mechanism, protected by the system sandbox.
- Deleting the app will permanently erase all associated data.

### 5. User Choices & Control

You may:
- Delete the app from iOS Settings to remove all data at any time.
- Reset game progress from the in-app menu.

### 6. Children's Privacy

WhatsCode is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app notice or a website announcement.
